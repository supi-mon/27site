Services

Dedicated team

27 technologies houses dedicated implementation team for clients, working closely with their own internal IT team much like an extension of it.

Benefits being:

Outourced model

27 also works on a project basis where the company acts as a consulting team for the Digital agencies. Instead of working directly with client, 27 instead focuses on working with digital agencies who require implementation team for their projects.

The choice to stay away from evolving into a digital agency is intentional as we do not want to focus on UX visualisation skills which is a specialisation. We instead would like to channelise our UI/UX skills to develop our own products, thereby having to only deal with one client (ourselves) rather than a lot of them.


Unlike the earlier model where the client is responsible for everything, on this model however 27 leads the way and is responsible for the following:

  • Implement / Advice cutting edge solution for the idea / problem
  • Focus on website performance on devices and search engines
  • Comply with GDPR and advice / implement testing standards
  • Implement / enable data driven strategies using Analytics
  • Work on Scrum model for incorporating changes on the fly as well as delivering on time
  • Support, maintain and train the client for autonomous functioning
  • Set long term technology transformation goals so that the present solution doesn't turn obselete in the near futre
