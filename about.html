about 27

Our mission is to create better customer experiences by making quality web delivery accessible and cost-effective to all companies.




the start of something new.

27 is a web services company partnering with growth-oriented agencies and organizations on the implementation side. From developing enterprise content management systems, ecommerce applications, microservices to dashboard applications we have garnered enough experience to blend in, guide and lead the implementation team for our partners.

27 was formed in the year 2013 as a dedicated development unit for 77Agency, a well established new media marketing agency based out of Europe. The company eventually partnered with other agencies, primarily in Europe to provide development services before exploring product development and training.

Today we are a team of 15 developers who strive to make a difference on the web.

How we get it done

We have an intensive traininig unit in existence within the company where we hire freshers and roll out fresh batches of developers each year. This significantly helps reduce costs for our clients.

In order to assure quality though, we associate with consultants from time to time as needed, similar to the pay-as-you-go model on cloud.
